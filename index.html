About

Richard Davies has been defending the rights of people charged with DUI/DWI and other crimes in Port Townsend and Jefferson County for over 20 years.

Mr. Davies graduated from the University of Washington School of Law in 1988. After graduating, he moved to the southwest, where he worked as a government attorney doing complex federal civil litigation.
