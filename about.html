DUI, TRAFFIC & CRIMINAL DEFENSE

Defending people in Port Townsend for over 20 Years

Richard Davies — Attorney at Law

Richard Davies has been defending the rights of people charged with DUI/DWI and other crimes in Port Townsend and Jefferson County for over 20 years.

Focusing exclusively on DUI, traffic and criminal defense, Richard Davies' clients benefit from his sound legal advice, knowledge of the local system, and extensive trial experience.

His breadth of criminal defense experience includes dozens of felony and misdemeanor jury trials, and appeals before the Court of Appeals and the State Supreme Court. He is often consulted by local and out-of-town attorneys about how to handle DUI/DWI, traffic and criminal cases in Jefferson County courts.

Mr. Davies graduated from the University of Washington School of Law in 1988. After graduating, he moved to the southwest, where he worked as a government attorney doing complex federal civil litigation.

Since moving to Port Townsend in 1993, Mr. Davies has focussed on DUI, traffic, and criminal defense.

Richard Davies is a member of:
  • Washington State Bar Association
  • Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals
  • United States District Court, Western District of Washington
  • Washington Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers
  • Washington Defender Association
