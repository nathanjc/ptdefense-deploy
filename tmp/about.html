Meet Richard Davies — Attorney at Law

Richard Davies has been defending the rights of people charged with DUI/DWI and other crimes in Port Townsend and Jefferson County for over 17 years.

Mr. Davies graduated from the University of Washington School of Law in 1988. After graduating, he moved to the southwest, where he worked as a government attorney doing complex federal civil litigation.

Richard Davies is a member of:
  • Washington State Bar Association
  • Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals
  • United States District Court, Western District of Washington
  • Washington Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers
  • Washington Defender Association
  • American Civil Liberties Union
  • National Organization for the Reform of Marijuana Laws
